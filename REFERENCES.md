# References And Source Material

This repository does not vendor the article PDF. The published article is copyrighted by The Review of Economic Studies/Oxford University Press, and a public GitHub repository should avoid redistributing the PDF unless the license explicitly permits it.

Primary references:

- Primiceri, Giorgio E. (2005). "Time Varying Structural Vector Autoregressions and Monetary Policy." *The Review of Economic Studies*, 72(3), 821-852. https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1467-937X.2005.00353.x
- Del Negro, Marco, and Giorgio E. Primiceri (2015). "Time Varying Structural Vector Autoregressions and Monetary Policy: A Corrigendum." *The Review of Economic Studies*, 82(4), 1342-1345. https://doi.org/10.1093/restud/rdv024
- Author-hosted working-paper PDF: https://faculty.wcas.northwestern.edu/gep575/tvsvar_final_july_04.pdf
- Corrigendum PDF and appendix: https://faculty.wcas.northwestern.edu/gep575/ErrataFinal4.pdf and https://faculty.wcas.northwestern.edu/gep575/ErrataFinal4appendix.pdf

Related public code/data:

- `bvarsv`, an R/C++ implementation of the Primiceri model with the 2015 corrigendum incorporated: https://cran.r-project.org/package=bvarsv
- `bvarsv` GitHub repository: https://github.com/FK83/bvarsv
- The `bvarsv` package includes `usmacro`, a U.S. macro dataset for 1953Q1-2001Q3 with inflation, unemployment, and the Treasury bill rate.
